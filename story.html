About Me

MY STORY

From Dar es Salaam to building digital products for East Africa — the journey of a landscape architect, developer and founder.

The Journey

HOW IT ALL STARTED

2003

BORN IN TANZANIA

Born on 11th March 2003 in Tanzania. Grew up with a natural curiosity about how spaces are designed and how people interact with their environment. That early curiosity laid the foundation for everything that followed.

2023

ARDHI UNIVERSITY · LANDSCAPE ARCHITECTURE

Enrolled in BSc Landscape Architecture at Ardhi University, Dar es Salaam. Here I discovered the power of design to address environmental challenges. I became passionate about climate-responsive urban landscapes, coastal design and how architecture can answer Africa's urban growth challenges.

2023

FOUNDED THE GREEN SHIFT MAGAZINE

Launched The Green Shift Magazine — promoting sustainable landscape architecture and climate solutions in Africa. This was my first step into media and editorial work, covering green cities, coastal afforestation, and design principles for a resilient future.

2024

CO-FOUNDED SMARTSCAPE ARCHITECTURE

Co-founded SmartScape Architecture — a platform for education, advocacy and innovation in landscape architecture across Africa. We produce content and resources advancing the field of landscape practice on the continent.

2025

INDUSTRIAL TRAINING & ARCHITECTURE PROJECTS

Completed industrial training at MK ARCHPLAN (Moshi) and SAPA Construction (Dar es Salaam). During this period I designed and rendered major projects including a football stadium, Islamic center, luxurious bungalows and commercial complex landscapes — all using ArchiCAD and Lumion.

2026

FOUNDED TRAVEX DIGITAL GROUP (TDG)

Founded Travex Digital Group — a digital ventures company building marketplace and fintech products for East African SMEs. Launched Travex Mall (Africa's intelligent digital marketplace) and Travex Finance (financial management SaaS). Currently leading product vision, full-stack development and business strategy across the TDG ecosystem.

2026

TRIPLE A ARCHITECTS · INTERN LANDSCAPE ARCHITECT

Worked as an Intern Landscape Architect at Triple A Architects in Dar es Salaam (January – June 2026). Produced site plans, floor plans, sections and landscape proposals for residential, mosque and sports facility projects.
