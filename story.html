About Me

MY STORY

From Dar es Salaam to building digital products and designing spaces for East Africa.

The Journey

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

I was born on 11th March 2003 in Tanzania. From a young age, I was drawn to two worlds that most people see as completely separate — the physical world of space and design, and the digital world of technology and systems. That curiosity never left me.

In 2023, I enrolled at Ardhi University to study BSc Landscape Architecture in Dar es Salaam. It was here that everything clicked. I discovered that design is not just about aesthetics — it is about solving problems, shaping communities, and responding to the environment. I became passionate about climate-responsive urban landscapes, coastal design, and how architecture can answer Africa's rapid urban growth challenges.

That same year, I founded The Green Shift Magazine — a publication promoting sustainable landscape architecture and climate solutions across Africa. It covers green cities, coastal afforestation, and design principles for a resilient future. Not long after, I co-founded SmartScape Architecture, a platform for education, advocacy and innovation in landscape architecture.

My industrial training took me to MK ARCHPLAN in Moshi, Kilimanjaro, and SAPA Construction in Dar es Salaam. Six weeks at each firm — drafting, site work, project documentation. These were the months I designed what became my proudest project: a full football stadium, complete with spectator stands, pitch layout, landscape buffer zones and access circulation. Alongside it came the Islamic Center, luxurious bungalow designs, a commercial complex landscape, and a luxury hotel — all rendered in Live Home 3D with full site planning.

In early 2026, I took the leap into entrepreneurship and founded Travex Digital Group (TDG) — Tanzania's pioneering digital ventures company. The question that drove me was simple: why are so many talented Tanzanian businesses invisible to the world? The answer wasn't a lack of effort — it was a lack of access to the right tools. TDG was my answer.

I built and shipped Travex Mall — Africa's intelligent digital marketplace — and Travex Finance, a financial management SaaS for Tanzanian retail businesses. Both are live today, serving real people across Tanzania. As CEO and Vision Lead, I lead product strategy, full-stack development, and business growth across the entire TDG ecosystem.

Between January and June 2026, I also served as an Intern Landscape Architect at Triple A Architects in Dar es Salaam — producing site plans, landscape proposals and architectural drawings across residential, mosque and sports facility typologies.

"I didn't build Travex to create a tech company. I built it to be a solution — a bridge between Tanzania's incredible human potential and the intelligent digital tools they deserve to succeed."

— Jumanne Maregeri, Founder & CEO, Travex Digital Group

Today I wear many hats — landscape architect, developer, founder, and author. I write magazines across architecture, lifestyle, sports, digital and tech. I believe that the most impactful work happens at the intersection of disciplines, and that East Africa's future will be built by people who refuse to be limited to just one box.
